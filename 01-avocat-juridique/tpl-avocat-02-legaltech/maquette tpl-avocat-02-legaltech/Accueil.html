Questions you might have

How is your billing different from traditional law firms? expand_more
We prioritize flat-fee billing for most services. This means you know exactly what you'll pay before we even start, eliminating the anxiety of billable hours and surprise invoices.
Do you offer remote consultations? expand_more
Yes, we offer fully secure video consultations and a digital portal for document sharing, making legal care accessible from the comfort of your own home.
What happens during the first consultation? expand_more
The first session is about listening. We'll discuss your goals, explain the legal landscape in plain English, and provide a roadmap with clear next steps and cost estimates.
