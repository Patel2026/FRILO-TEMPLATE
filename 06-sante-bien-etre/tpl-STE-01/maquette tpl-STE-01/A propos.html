Meet the Founder

Dr. Elena Sterling, Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Sterling founded The Clinical Sanctuary in 1998 with a singular vision: to strip away the intimidating facades of healthcare and replace them with a culture of transparency and tranquility.

With a background in both Neurosurgery and Integrative Medicine, she has pioneered the "Sanctuary Protocol"—a treatment philosophy that has been adopted by clinics worldwide for its exceptional success in long-term patient outcomes.
