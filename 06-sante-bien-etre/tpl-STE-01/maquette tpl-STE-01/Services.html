Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day appointments?

expand_more
Yes, we reserve several slots daily for urgent care needs. Please call our reception before 10:00 AM to secure a same-day consultation.

How can I access my lab results?

expand_more
All laboratory results are uploaded securely to our Patient Portal within 24-48 hours. You will receive a secure notification once they are ready for review.

Which insurance providers do you work with?

expand_more
We work with most major private insurance providers including Blue Cross, Aetna, and Cigna. Please check our "Insurance" page for a full updated list of accepted plans.

Can I consult with a specialist directly?

expand_more
While many of our specialists accept direct bookings, some may require a referral from your Primary Care Physician. Our team can guide you through this process.
