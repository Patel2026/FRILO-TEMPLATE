The Farm-to-Buffet Philosophy

Freshness is our only ingredient that isn't negotiable.

Founded by the Harrison family in 2014, our journey began with a simple question: Why should fine, farm-fresh produce be limited to small plates? We reimagined the buffet as a celebration of abundance, where quality and variety live in harmony.
