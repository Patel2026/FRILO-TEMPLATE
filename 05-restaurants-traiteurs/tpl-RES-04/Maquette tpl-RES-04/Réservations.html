Important information regarding your arrival at the Sanctuary.

Dress Code

add

Cyber-chic, avant-garde, or high-end evening wear is encouraged. No casual sportswear, flip-flops, or heavy industrial workwear. Management reserves the right to refuse entry based on attire.

Age Requirement

add

Electric Sanctuary is strictly 21+. Valid government-issued photo ID is required for all guests. Digital copies or photos of IDs are not accepted under any circumstances.

add
