Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Refinement

Founded in 1994, The Editorial Aura began as a private atelier dedicated to the art of dermatological excellence. Today, we stand as a beacon of clinical beauty, where heritage meets the frontier of aesthetic innovation.

Our journey is one of quiet evolution. We believe that true luxury is found in the details—the precise calibration of a laser, the silent grace of our treatment rooms, and the profound expertise of our practitioners.
